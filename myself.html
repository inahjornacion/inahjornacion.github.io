Myself


Hello. How are you? How's college so far? How's the girl who have been fighting and struggling to overcome her battles? I hope you're doing fine.

To start off, I want to say that I am proud of you. Things have been tough and hard for you lately, but we will manage to keep our heads up and continue fighting whatever happens. I am proud that you have made it this far and what you have become. Please don't be so hard to yourself. Everything that is happening to you has a reason. You may not yet see it now, but someday it will make sense. All the pain, struggles, and frustration will be worth it. You are not lost. You are exactly where you are meant to be. Trust God's timing and plans for you. Be patient, resilient, and at the same time, relax.

You are perfect in every way despite of your flaws. Even when you are hurting, there is beauty in your pain. You are a lot stronger now for you have overcame the challenges that tried to tear you down. I know that you are trying your best and we will claim victory when the time comes.

You are smart. You are beautiful. You are enough. You are everything. Thank you for never giving up and being so tough. I love you.
